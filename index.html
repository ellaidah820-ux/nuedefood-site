Simply browse our menu, add items to your basket, fill in your delivery details, and proceed to checkout. You'll receive your order confirmation via WhatsApp after payment.

What are your delivery areas and times?

We deliver within Abuja town only. Delivery times are between 8AM - 10PM daily. Orders must be placed a day ahead for next-day delivery.
